Privacy Policy

Strength Log — a personal training dashboard · last updated July 2026

Strength Log is a personal, single-user project used by its owner to view their own training and health data. It is not a commercial product and has no other users.

What data it uses

With your explicit authorization via WHOOP's OAuth flow, the app reads your WHOOP recovery, sleep, cycle (strain) and workout data, plus basic profile info, through the official WHOOP API. It requests read-only access.

How it's used

The data is fetched on a daily schedule and displayed on a personal dashboard at archievadher-sudo.github.io/strength-log, alongside strength-training and running logs. It is used only to visualise your own progress and recovery.

Storage & sharing

The fetched data is stored as a JSON file in the project's source repository and shown on the dashboard, which is publicly viewable. The data is not sold, and not shared with any third party beyond being retrieved from WHOOP and rendered on the dashboard. No analytics or trackers are used.

Revoking access

You can revoke the app's access at any time from your WHOOP account settings; the app will then stop receiving new data.

Contact

Questions about this project can be directed to the repository owner via github.com/archievadher-sudo/strength-log.

This policy applies solely to the personal Strength Log application and its use of the WHOOP API.
